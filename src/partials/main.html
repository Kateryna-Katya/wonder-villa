Match in Style at Wonder Villa

Uncover hidden tiles, decorate chic spaces, and enjoy puzzle serenity like never before

Game Description

Wonder Villa is the coziest tile match game where design meets puzzle fun. Relax, match tiles, and make your dream villa shine with style!

How to Play

  • Tap to Match

    Select three matching tiles to clear them.

  • Use Power-Ups

    Boosters help you in tricky levels.

  • Decorate Rooms

    Complete puzzles to unlock design choices.

  • Progress Through Levels

    Each challenge gets harder – and more rewarding!

  • Relax and Enjoy

    Cozy aesthetics make gameplay soothing.

Design Your Dream Villa

Step into the designer’s seat! With every level you complete, unlock new furniture, color palettes, and room layouts. From boho chic to classic elegance — your villa, your style.
